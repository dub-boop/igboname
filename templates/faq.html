FAQ

What is it about?

The Afamefuna Project is a not for profit, open source, platform with interest in the area of Onomastics - particularly dedicated to curating, understanding, promoting Igbo names in fun relatable ways. This is why www.myigboname.com exists.

"What have we done?"

We have over 3000 entries and more awaiting approval. The little passion project has grown from little beginnings but we havenâ€™t done it alone. From culture enthusiasts, linguists, and every Emeka Okeke, community members have chipped in to bring the project this far. But it is still work in progress.

"Can I work with you?"

Yes. We have worked in the past with undergraduates in the field of linguistics, we continue to encourage and appreciate the input of culture enthusiasts and linguists who may be willing to contribute new names or write articles for the blog. But we will also require the contributions of web developers and designers. Web developers who wish to contribute to the improvement of this platform may contact us using the contribute section.

"Do you offer paid employment"

Unfortunately, we do not have the capacity to offer payment for services at this moment. We have in the past provided stipend for undergraduate who helped in the project but we cannot at this present moment.
